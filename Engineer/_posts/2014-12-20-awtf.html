Work:

The scope of this project was to build an Advanced Water Treatment Facility that would take in secondary effluent from an adjacent wastewater treatment plant and subject it to a purification process through the use of microfiltration, reverse osmosis and disinfection with ultraviolet light. This facility treats 70 million gallons of water per day, which are then used to recharge the aquifer and create a seawater intrusion barrier.

My Role

My position for this project was Mechanical Engineer assigned to the reverse osmosis, ultraviolet light and decarbonation buildings. I reviewed submittals and procured materials and equipment, participated in preparation of work plans, and supervised field installation. Once field installation was complete, I transitioned into commissioning and became part of the team that successfully started-up the facility
